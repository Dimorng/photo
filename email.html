**** Dental Quiz will go through Kahoot and ZOOM ****

APDSA Annual Congress’s Dental Quiz will be using 2 mains (Kahoot and ZOOM) application to facility from the beginning till the end.

Kahoot! is a cloud-based quiz platform that we use during Dental Quiz in Round I (MCQ quiz, Multiple Choice Questions) and Round II (Diagnosis Quiz of Clinical Cases). The accuracy and duration of the answers will be calculated automatically in Kahoot scoring system, and it also live on ZOOM for non-competition participants as well.

ZOOM, is use for live from Round I till Round III Final stage of the competition. Especially, in Round III both semi-final and final (debate session) are arrange for participants to compete in ZOOM.
